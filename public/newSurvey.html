Step 1: Let's get started

One project might contain several surveys. Naming your projects will make it easier for you to quickly find your different surveys in the overview. The project title won't be visible to participants. The survey title is the official name of your survey and will be visible to all participants.

Step 2: Demographic Filters

Collecting demographic data will help you to filter your survey results based on the age, gender income and other factors. Please select those demographic factors that will be relevant for your survey. Don't worry - you will be able to adapt the wording of your selected demographic questions in the next step.

Step 3: Add Questions

This is where the real fun begins. You are now able to add as many questions to your survey as you like. If you want to take a break editing, you can always save your survey as a draft and come back at a later time.

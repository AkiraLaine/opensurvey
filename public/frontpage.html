MEET YOUR NEW DASHBOARD.

All your data in one place. Your dashboard allows you to quickly browse through collected replies, displaying both visual graphs and raw data. It doesn’t matter if you are conducting a quick poll between friends or a sophisticated study for your dissertation, multiple viewing modes of the dashboard will allow you to show the information you really care about and hide everything else. Need to dive in even deeper? One click exports your data into csv files compatible with excel, google docs, open calc and other spreadsheet programs.

Ask the right questions

Choose between three distinct question types: Open questions, to gain qualitative data and let your participants give individual text-based answers. Multiple Choice questions, that will present your participants with several options to choose from and numerical scale questions, allowing you to measure attitudes and opinions and converting those directly to numbers for statistical evaluation. The choice is yours.

Find the right answers

Easily filter your responses depending on demographical factors such as age, gender and income of the participants. Adding and removing multiple filters is as easy as clicking a single button, so you can focus on spotting the patterns and variance of your datasets.

100% Open Source

Let’s work together. Fork the project on Github or raise issues in the repository. By collaborating, we can make the application more reliable, add further question types, filters, features, design choices and more to create a free and open standard for digital surveys that can compete with larger commercial offerings. With that thought in mind, the software is released under GLPv3, meaning you can use it as a foundation for your personal or commercial projects for free.
